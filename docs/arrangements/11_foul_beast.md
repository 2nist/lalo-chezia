## Arrangement Philosophy

This first part of the suite represents the album's descent into darker, more aggressive territory. "Foul Beast" serves as an exploration of inner turmoil and destructive patterns, using dissonance and aggression to convey emotional intensity. The arrangement builds from unsettling beginnings to maximum chaos, creating a powerful and intense listening experience that pushes the boundaries of the album's sonic palette.
