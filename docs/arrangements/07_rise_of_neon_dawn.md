## Arrangement Philosophy

This song serves as the album's anthem, representing awakening and triumph. The arrangement builds from driving beginnings to maximum intensity, using the E major key to create a sense of brightness and victory. The "neon dawn" concept is reflected in the bright, synthetic textures combined with organic rock elements, creating a modern anthem that feels both futuristic and timeless.
