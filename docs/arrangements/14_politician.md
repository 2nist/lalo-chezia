## Arrangement Philosophy

This drum and bass cover of "Politician" represents the album's most experimental and processed interpretation. The arrangement transforms the original into a glitch-heavy, rhythmically complex piece that maintains the song's essence while pushing it into new sonic territory. The use of glitch effects and heavy processing creates a modern, experimental take on the classic while maintaining the driving energy and political undertones of the original.
